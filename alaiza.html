Welcome to Bukidnon

Known as the "Food Basket of Mindanao," Bukidnon is a captivating province in Northern Mindanao, Philippines. With its cool climate, vast agricultural fields, and majestic natural landscapes, it offers a myriad of experiences for nature lovers, adventurers, and culture enthusiasts alike.

Explore the wonders of Bukidnon through its popular destinations, rich culture, and breathtaking scenery.

About Bukidnon

Bukidnon boasts a diverse cultural heritage from indigenous groups including the Higaonon, Manobo, and Talaandig peoples. The province thrives on agriculture producing corn, pineapple, coffee, and rice, supporting one of the richest provinces in the country.

Environmental initiatives focus on forest restoration and wildlife preservation, including efforts to protect the endangered Philippine Eagle. Bukidnon is also known for its festivals, such as the colorful Kaamulan Festival celebrating the indigenous tribes' traditions and unity.
